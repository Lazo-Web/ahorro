# **App Name**: ScanShop Saver

## Core Features:

- Barcode Scanner Integration: Utilize the device's camera to scan product barcodes, linking them to product information.
- Pantry Management: Maintain a dynamic inventory of household goods, updated by barcode scans.
- AI-Powered Budget Tool: The LLM takes into account scanned purchases to predict monthly spending on groceries, providing opportunities for savings and smart alternatives based on historical data.
- Shopping List Generation: Generate shopping lists based on pantry levels and desired recipes, aiding in efficient grocery shopping.
- Expense Tracker: Categorize and track spending on scanned items.
- Multiple Supermarket Support: Allow users to log where they bought what item. Use the user-provided supermarket to better estimate cost of replacement goods.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep Indigo (#3F51B5), reflecting trustworthiness and efficiency.
- Background color: Very light Indigo (#E8EAF6), nearly white, for a clean and unobtrusive backdrop.
- Accent color: Violet (#7986CB) provides visual contrast.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif, to give a modern but inviting feel to all text elements.
- Consistent, minimalistic icons to represent categories of products, pantry items, and options, ensuring a user-friendly experience.
- Clean, grid-based layout, offering easy navigation and seamless interaction with lists and product information.
- Subtle transitions and animations on list items, menu appearances, and barcode scanning feedback to provide intuitive cues.
